Privacy Policy for BTN Sport Informer

Effective Date: April 14, 2024

Welcome to BTN Sport Informer! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we do not collect, share, or store, and how we use third-party APIs to provide our services.

Information We Do Not Collect

BTN Sport Informer does not collect, store, or share any personal information of its users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Usage data
  • Device information

Our app is designed to be used without the need for any personal data from our users.

Use of Third-party APIs

To provide up-to-date sports information, BTN Sport Informer utilizes several third-party APIs. These APIs are used solely to fetch sports-related data, such as scores, news, and event information, to display within the app. We ensure that our use of these APIs complies with their respective privacy policies and does not involve the collection, storage, or sharing of our users' personal information.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

BTN Sport Informer reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Such modifications will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy within the app or on any related website. We encourage our users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the information we do not collect.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us through the support features provided in the app.

Thank you for choosing BTN Sport Informer. We are excited to provide you with a secure and privacy-focused sports information experience!
